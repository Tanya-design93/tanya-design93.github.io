Our story

The Company was founded in 2018 in Ottawa as a family business. Our main factor of success was that our donuts were cooked every day and we provided a large variety of choices. After which, we had begun to advertise our cafe on social media. After our success in social media, in 2019, we opened our first restaurant!

Our restaurant became a unique place due to our impressive variety, and as a result, we have a tremendous amount of amazing clients from around the world. Moreover, we utilize new technology to help bake with all natural products. Since 2020, We met a new milestone within our company, we now offer the ability for our customers to customize their own donuts for their merry celebrations. We know our business will be growing with our wonderful customer base.
